$OM
Exchange Rates
Dec 2019 - July 2020
App Store
$OM is an application for reviewing exchange rates and calculating SOM (National currency of Kyrgyz Republic) into foreign currencies. Designed by techies for techies. The product is entirely non-profit and will always remain so, that is, free to use and without any advertising integrations.

Onboarding

Walkthrough by main points
Setup default currency and pick off non-relevant exchange points

Easy swipe

Just 3 screen for everything
Swipe as the best way for fast navigating

Exchange rates

Collection view for currencies
List of exchange points depends on the rolling currencies block. User can change the ordering of list according to his needs

Converter

Enter the required amount
Converter can be also an as isolated feature where user be able instantly change currency type or change to another exchange point

Settings

Customize for yourself
User be able change default currency and include or exclude exchange points

Light mode

Re-pick color mode
Application can handle both color modes. Even if user want to change color mode different from the one selected in iOS

HOMS
Social Network
Mar 2019 - Apr 2020
homs.by
HOMS - the private social network for neighborhoods. It works as a hub for trusted connections and the exchange of helpful information, goods, and services. Building connections in the real world is a universal human need. HOMS brings unique features like a dialing by car number if someone blocks you in the parking lot.

Onboarding

Getting know the app
Easy sign up via entering address and user will be taken to the appropriate community. In special cases, confirmation is required

Feed

Getting orderliness
Each message reflects one problem in a certain subject within which dialogue can be conducted instead of messful feed in WhatsApp groups

Direct message

Resolve personal issues
Having a specific question? User be able to send a direct message to neighbors. Whole dialogue is private

Context menu

Discover unique features
HOMS contains special features besides sharing information. Blocked in the parking lot? So call the owner, knowing only the car number

Profile

Manage personal data
Switch between the private communities of which you are a member. Keep up to date your personal information
And many many more…
Application has more than 120 screens and states designed for iOS and Android

QOOVEE
B2B e-Commerce
July 2017 - Mar 2018
qoovee.com
Qoovee — is a ecosystem of B2B platforms in wholesale e-Commerce. "Market" for demonstrating products, "Orders" to place specific requests, "Services" to find relevant contractors for handling commercial or something else issues, "Intellect" for resolving legal issues in trade and "Logistics" for transporting goods.

Onboarding

Guided tour
As a multi-platform service, a user can choose required one for his case. As B2B service, also important to give possibility to choose a role and accompany further based on the selected

Main page

See big picture
Discover the full range possibilities of the platform by the considered and aesthetic layout. Taking attention, making to be interested, creating a desire, and calling to action.

Description

Trace all the way
Each service explained as much as possible on internal pages. User can research every site of service capabilities

Subscriptions

Individual feed
Customize your business interests for yourself instead of looking for products in endless categories

Goods

Familiar look
The adapted layout of traditional B2P e-commerce internal of goods for wholesale products with relevant information
Except for the service "Market", We have designed with my team the services “Orders”, “Intellect”, and “Logistics”. A titanic amount of work resulted in countless pages

Application

B2B wholesale in your pocket
All Qoovee ecosystem were challenged and implemented to put in an appropriate way to the mobile environment

Subutai Bazaar
Cloud Market
Jul 2015 - Nov 2016
bazaar.subutai.io
The products of SaaS that propose virtual machines for hosting are expensive and not flexible for small business and personal use. The Subutai Bazaar is a marketplace that allows anyone to instantly buy, share, or rent computer resources, as well as raise and operate a sharing economy for applications.

Resource machines

Market as it is
Choose required machines called as the peers. Users are able to sort them by any relevant parameters

Environment creation

Drag & drop templates
Inside peers placed predefined software in containers that the user can install according to his case for deploying a mobile application, server, or whatever...

War room

Track the metrics
War room combining all statistical data and user feedback for a clear situation of growing service for stakeholders
And many many more…
A total of 300 pages were designed for this project
